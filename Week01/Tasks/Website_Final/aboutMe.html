About Me

Hey, my name is Taylor. I'm originally from Ossining, NY, home to the beautiful Sing Sing maximum security prison, located along the scenic Hudson River.

A particularly spooky fact: I was born on October 31st, 1994, in Sleep Hollow, NY, less than a mile from where Washington Irving himself is buried. Other famous dead people buried here include Andrew Carnegie and Walter Crystler.

As enthralling as all that death is, I decided to broaden my horizons and moved a whole three hours to the expansive state of Rhode Island, where I spent five years studying electrical engineering and German at the Unviersity of Rhode Island in the made-up town of Kingston. As part of the International Engineering Program, I also spent a year living in Germany, where I studied and worked, in between drinking many liters of beer and eating large pretzels while in lederhosen. Quite the experience.

As if my life wasn't thrilling enough, I also have time for hobbies, which you can read about here.
