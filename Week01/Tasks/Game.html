Make 15

Make 15 is a two player game. Take turns clicking numbers with a friend.

Rules:
Players alternate turns choosing numbers. Once a player has chosen a number, it cannot be chosen by the other player. The first player to have three distinct numbers that add up to exactly 15 wins! Draws are possible.
