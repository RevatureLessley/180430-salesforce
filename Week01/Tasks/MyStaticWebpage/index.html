Who Am I?

  • The first PC I touched was a TRS-80 Radio Shack computer. My high school bought two in my junior year in preparation for teaching different subjects. I reverse engineered a simple Pac-Man type game and learned about program logic in the Basic language using methods such as if conditions and for-next loops.
  • During my Navy career, I created a database for spare parts inventory to make it easier to find and re-order parts. My real discovery of my attraction to coding was at my last place of employement.
    After the company decided to move to a cheaper application to handle their 3M system, I was given the opportunity to develop an Access database in keeping with the accepted standards of the desired functionability of the previous 3M system.
  • Getting my degree was the best decision I have ever made and now I am using my degree to become a software developer at Revature.

I'm an old man with a mission!
