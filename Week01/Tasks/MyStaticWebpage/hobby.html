Hobbies and Interests

My main hobby is video games, but that's boring to talk about. Instead, I'll talk about a more fun thing to talk about, sand sculpture.
I can say I started sand sculpture when I was in high school. My dad took me along the gigs and events he participated in along with our sand sculpture team Archisand since I was a little kid.
Needless to say, I didn't contribute much at first besides wrecking the sculpture after it was finished. However, over time I started contributing more and more.
Since large-scale sand sculpture requires metric tons of sand to be shoveled into forms, infused with water, and compacted, child manual labor was generally accepted with open arms.
Naturally, as an impoverished child laborer, I worked long hours of toiling in the sand with glee, knowing my efforts would fund my video game expenditures.
