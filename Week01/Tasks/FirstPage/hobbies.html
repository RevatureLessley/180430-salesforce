Hello, welcome to my hobbies page!

I have been obsessed with the sport of Olympic Weightlifting for about 8 years now. Olympic weightlifting only involves two lifts:

  1. 1) Clean & Jerk
  2. 2) Snatch
I have a deep respect for the sport of Olympic Weightlifting because it encompasses all of the aspects of athleticism that I value such as Strength, Technique, Flexibility, Power and Focus.

Virtually every gold medalist competing in the olympics has been practicing from a very young age.
